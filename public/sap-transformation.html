Coverage areas

The transformation spans three enterprise domains, each with its own module footprint, legal-entity complexity, and downstream reporting dependencies.

Finance

GL, AP/AR, close, consolidations, intercompany, multi-bank connectivity, payroll costing and cross-company charging.

Supply Chain

Procure-to-Pay, Plan-to-Deliver, master data alignment, and integration to logistics/satellite systems.

Human Resources

Payroll archiving, role cleanup, and HR data structure changes tied into the S/4HANA target model.

Program roadmap & milestones

A two-year transformation with a multi-release roadmap and a disciplined cutover cadence.

Aug 2023

Program Kickoff

Aberdeen mobilized ~10–15 team members to lead strategy, governance, and delivery.

2024–2025

Build & Integrate

Target architecture built out, data & integration workstreams executed, satellite decommissioning underway.

RISE E4Q

Environment Delivered

SAP RISE E4Q environment successfully stood up for downstream testing.

Through Feb 2026

UAT & Mock Cutovers

User Acceptance Testing and Mock Cutover 3 executed, dress rehearsal planning in flight.

May 2026

Production Go-Live

Planned production cutover to SAP S/4HANA on RISE.
